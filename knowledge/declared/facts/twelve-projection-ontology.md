Grafema 0.3 ontology defines 12 projections of the sociotechnical system:

1. Semantic (code structure) — fully code-derivable
2. Operational (execution) — partially code-derivable
3. Causal (incidents) — NOT code-derivable
4. Contractual (SLOs, tests) — partially code-derivable
5. Intentional (why it exists) — NOT code-derivable
6. Organizational (who is responsible) — NOT code-derivable
7. Temporal (when and in what order) — partially code-derivable
8. Epistemic (what is known) — NOT code-derivable
9. Security (access control) — partially code-derivable
10. Financial (cost) — NOT code-derivable
11. Behavioral (actual usage) — NOT code-derivable
12. Risk (what could go wrong) — NOT code-derivable

Three soundness levels: formal (code analysis), heuristic (git-derived),
declared (manual/API).

Source: "Beyond Code: The Sociotechnical Context Graph" position paper.
